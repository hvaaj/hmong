Intro / Instructions

There is 4 tshooj, then sau (end) which ended the Dee,

Then you blow your zaaj (personal song) Then you blow first half of dee sau, Then blow zaaj again Then you blow first half of dee sau along with the second half of dee Sau. This is the complete first cycle.

You blow this cycle a total of 3 times.

This is what people blow at the funeral during downtime when guest blows their personal zaaj.

When u have never blown qeej and don’t know what to listen for, than it seems like they are blowing everything different but actually, it’s just a repetition of 1 to 2 things only.

Currently, mark has tshooj 1,2,3, and sau, and is in the process of learning a zaaj. Then last thing he needs is dee sau first half and second half then he would be able to blow at the funeral.

When you blow tshooj 1,2,3,4 and sau, then u don’t repeat those anymore

You go into your zaaj, dee sau first half, zaaj, dee sau first half and second half to end (sau)

Jump right into zaaj, dee sau 1st half, zaaj, dee sau 2nd half to sau

Last round jump into zaaj again, dee sai 1st half, zaaj, dee sau 2nd half to sau
