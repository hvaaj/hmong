Common Items, Terms, Phrases, Etiquette

Common Event Order
When hu plig or Ua neeg for example
You should have the family talk first before giving thanks.
Let the visitors know why they are here before they give money or their blessing

Common Etiquette
Handing someone at the feast table a folk, spoon or knife
You must hand them the handle. Never the end that pooks
For example when you use the folk to folk a peice of meat you want to give them the handle so they can grab it.

After handing them the folk, hand them cup(s) of beer. Usually two cups

proper way to bow, kev cai pe
When you bow, does not matter the event, whether you are thanking them or asking for help for example to paab ua neeb. You always bow towards the xwm kaab.
